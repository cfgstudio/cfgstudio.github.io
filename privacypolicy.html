Privacy Policy | CFG Studio

Last Updated: 04-03-2026

CFG Studio ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our games.

By downloading or using our games, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information Collected Automatically

When you use our games, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version
  • Unique device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA where applicable)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate location)
  • Country and language
  • App version and installation data
  • Gameplay data and in-game events
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

b) Account Information (Future Feature)

We may implement account systems using PlayFab in future updates. If accounts are introduced, data such as player ID, login credentials (if applicable), and gameplay progress may be stored securely through PlayFab services.

c) In-App Purchases

Our games offer in-app purchases processed through Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment card information. Payment data is handled directly by the respective platform provider.

2. Analytics and Performance Services

We use third-party analytics tools to improve gameplay experience and stability:

  • Firebase Analytics – usage statistics and behavior tracking
  • Firebase Crashlytics – crash reporting and diagnostics
  • Firebase Remote Config – dynamic configuration updates
  • GameAnalytics – gameplay analytics and performance insights

These services may collect anonymized or pseudonymized technical data including device information, game session length, events, and approximate location.

3. Advertising

Our games display advertisements through Google AdMob and mediation platforms, which may include multiple third-party ad networks.

Advertising partners may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID / Apple IDFA)
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location

This information is used to:

  • Provide personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

GDPR & Consent (EU/EEA/UK Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other applicable regions, we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain consent before serving personalized advertisements. Users may manage or withdraw consent at any time through in-app privacy settings where available.

Managing Advertising Preferences

Android: Settings → Google → Ads

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

We do not sell personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Advertising partners

  • Fraud prevention and security partners

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Your information may be processed and stored in countries outside your country of residence. By using our games, you consent to such transfers.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for analytics, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and service improvement purposes. Retention periods are governed by our third-party service providers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
